# Yapper — Product Strategy

## Phase 1: Free Web Tool (Current)

A free impromptu speaking practice tool on the web. Purpose: attract foot traffic and build an audience.

## Phase 2: Native iOS App — AI-Powered Content Creation for Speakers

**Core loop:** Inspiration → Ideation → Record → Feedback → Publish

### 1. Inspiration Database

- Connect Instagram & TikTok accounts to pull saved/liked content
- Import links, video URLs (auto-transcribed), and random ideas
- Voice-note quick capture — speak an idea and it's saved instantly

### 2. AI Ideation Engine

Takes raw inspirations and builds them into full content ideas:

- Hook options
- Main points & arguments
- Supporting stats
- Expanded talking points

Not a script — a structured ideation workflow. Ideas are surfaced to the user as video suggestions.

### 3. Video Recorder

Purpose-built for no-script, no-edit talking-head videos. Minimal, beautiful UI. User picks an idea, speaks to camera for as long as they want, done.

### 4. AI Speech Feedback

After recording, full transcript analysis with:

- **Visual feedback** — on-screen annotations/highlights
- **Audio feedback** — accent, tonality, word choice, delivery suggestions, alternative phrasing

Complete speech coaching in one step.

### 5. One-Click Publish

Post directly to Instagram and TikTok from the app.

## Monetization
